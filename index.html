Michaelangelo

Michaelangelo is a mutant turtle and a member of the infmaous TMNT. Michaelangelo is idetified by the orange mask and belt he wears and his dual nunchucks, his weapon of choice. He is also seen using a variety of weapons such as grappling hooks, tonfas and a three section staff.Michaelangelo can eat the most pizza and is most likely the laziest amongst the four.
